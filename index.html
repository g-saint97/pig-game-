  1. The game is played with two players.
  2. On each turn, a player rolls a six-sided die as many times as they want, and each roll's value is added to their turn total.
  3. However, if the player rolls a 1 on any of their rolls, their turn ends immediately, and they lose all the points they accumulated during that turn. It is then the other player's turn.
  4. A player can choose to "hold" their turn at any point, at which point their turn total is added to their overall score. It then becomes the other player's turn.
  5. The first player to reach a score of 100 or more points wins the game.
  6. If both players reach 100 points or more in the same turn, the player with the higher score wins.
  7. If a player rolls a 1 on their first roll of their turn, they score no points, and their turn ends immediately. It then becomes the other player's turn.

    8. These are the basic rules of the game. In the digital version of the game you linked to, there may be additional features or variations, but these rules should still apply.
